About Ikara Orphan Charity Trust

Empowering children and communities through compassion, education and sustainable development.

Our Story

Ikara Orphan Charity Trust (IOCT) was established to provide care, support and opportunities to vulnerable children and families. Through strategic interventions in education, healthcare, nutrition and empowerment, we help children achieve their full potential.

Mission & Vision

Mission

To improve the lives of vulnerable children through sustainable support systems.

Vision

A future where every child has access to education, healthcare and opportunity.
